PWR

Overview

    An internal regulator is embedded in the STM32F3xx devices.
  • The internal regulator is enabled in the STM32F30x MCUs: The STM32F30x devices require a 2.0 V - 3.6 V operating supply voltage (VDD) and a 2.0 V-3.6 V analog supply voltage (VDDA). The embedded regulator is used to supply the internal 1.8 V digital power.
  • The internal regulator is disabled in the STM32F31x MCUs: The STM32F31x devices require a 1.8 V +/- 8% operating voltage supply (VDD) and 1.65 V - 3.6 V analog voltage supply (VDDA). The embedded regulator is OFF and VDD directly supplies the regulator output.
