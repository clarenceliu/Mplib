I2S

Overview

    I2S features:
  • Full duplex communication
  • FMaster or slave operations
  • 8-bit programmable linear prescaler to reach accurate audio sample frequencies (from 8 kHz to 192 kHz)
  • Data format may be 16-bit, 24-bit or 32-bit
  • Underrun flag in slave transmission mode, overrun flag in reception mode (master and slave) and Frame Error Flag in reception and transmitter mode (slave only)
  • Data direction is always MSB first
  • DMA capability for transmission and reception (16-bit wide)
  • Master clock can be output to drive an external audio component. Ratio is fixed at 256 ¡Á FS(where FS is the audio sampling frequency)
  • I2S (I2S2 and I2S3) clock can be derived from an external clock mapped on the I2S_CKIN pin.
